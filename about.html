2006 Year Founded
Our Mission

Fighting for Every Child's Right to a Better Future

GSK Initiative was born out of a simple, urgent belief: no child should be left behind because of poverty, neglect, or circumstance. Since 2006, we have worked tirelessly on the streets, in communities, and at the highest levels of policy to transform that belief into reality.

We partner with families, governments, schools, and global organisations to deliver education, protection, reintegration, and empowerment for street-connected and vulnerable children across West Africa and beyond.

Our Journey

Two Decades of Impact

From a small community initiative to a nationally recognised charity — here is how our story has unfolded.

2006
Founded

GSK Initiative is Born

A small group of community advocates established GSK Initiative in Lagos, Nigeria, with a single goal: provide safe spaces and educational pathways for street-connected children.

2010
Expansion

Regional Growth & First Partnerships

We expanded operations into three additional states and forged our first formal partnerships with government welfare agencies. Our flagship education programme reached 800 children.

2015
Milestone

50 Successful Family Reintegrations

Our reintegration unit celebrated its 50th successful case — children returned safely to families and enrolled in school — a landmark that validated our holistic, child-centred model.

2020
Resilience

Serving Communities Through the Pandemic

When COVID-19 devastated vulnerable communities, we pivoted rapidly to deliver emergency food support to over 12 communities, ensuring no child went without during the crisis.

2026
Today

Global Coalition & Continued Growth

Today, GSK Initiative is a member of a 47-organisation global coalition on child rights, operates a new vocational centre in Ibadan, and continues to grow its reach across West Africa.

Listen First

We place children at the centre of every decision. Their voices, experiences, and aspirations directly shape our programmes, priorities, and approach. Nothing about them without them.

01

Focus on Impact

We direct our energy where change is measurable and lasting. We invest in proven approaches, rigorously evaluate outcomes, and are honest about what works and what doesn't.

02

Safety Always

Child welfare is non-negotiable. Every interaction, partnership, and programme is governed by strict safeguarding policies and international child protection standards.

03

Partnership

Lasting change requires collective effort. We work alongside governments, NGOs, families, and communities — sharing knowledge, resources, and responsibility for outcomes.

How We Work

A Model Built for Lasting Change

Our approach is grounded in years of fieldwork, research, and community feedback. We don't impose solutions — we co-create them with the people we serve, ensuring every programme is culturally appropriate, evidence-based, and sustainable long after we leave.

1

Community Needs Assessment

We begin by listening — engaging children, families, and local leaders to understand the real challenges before designing any response.

2

Tailored Programme Design

Programmes are designed specifically for each community context, combining international best practices with local knowledge and cultural sensitivity.

3

Implementation & Monitoring

Our trained field teams deliver programmes directly, with real-time monitoring to ensure quality, safety, and effectiveness at every stage.

4

Evaluation & Scale

We rigorously evaluate every intervention. Programmes that demonstrate impact are scaled; lessons from every initiative feed into continuous improvement.
